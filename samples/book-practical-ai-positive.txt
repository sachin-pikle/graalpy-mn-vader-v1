This book explains practical AI concepts in a way that feels clear, grounded, and surprisingly approachable.
The examples are small enough to follow, but still realistic enough to reuse in a real project.
I especially appreciated the chapters on tradeoffs, testing, and deployment because they avoid hype and focus on useful decisions.
The writing is sharp, the pacing is steady, and the diagrams support the text instead of distracting from it.
I would happily recommend it to engineers who want a confident introduction without unnecessary complexity.
