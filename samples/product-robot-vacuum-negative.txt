I wanted this robot vacuum to save time, but it has been frustrating from the first week.
It gets stuck under the couch, misses obvious crumbs along the edges, and often returns to the dock before finishing one room.
The app is slow, the mapping is unreliable, and the cleaning results are inconsistent.
I spend more time rescuing and resetting it than I would spend vacuuming by hand.
For the price, this product feels disappointing and unfinished.
